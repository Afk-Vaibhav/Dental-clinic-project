Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Simply click the "Book Appointment Now" button above to fill out our online form. You can also call us directly at +91 98765 43210.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring any previous dental records, X-rays, and a valid ID. If you have dental insurance, bring your insurance card as well.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, we do accept walk-ins. However, scheduled appointments receive priority to minimize your waiting time.

How long is a typical consultation?

Initial consultations typically last 30-45 minutes, allowing Dr. Rai to thoroughly examine your dental health and discuss treatment options.
